<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fiber Bragg Grating Amplifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-band Erbium-doped Fiber Amplifier (EDFA)</li><li>L-band Raman Fiber Amplifier (RFA)</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Bragg Grating amplifiers include C-band Erbium-doped Fiber Amplifiers (EDFAs) and L-band Raman Fiber Amplifiers (RFAs). EDFAs operate in the C-band wavelength range and are commonly used for long-haul communication systems. RFAs, on the other hand, operate in the L-band wavelength range and offer advantages in terms of reduced signal distortion and improved transmission performance. The hybrid market involves the integration of both EDFAs and RFAs to optimize network performance and address specific requirements in different wavelength ranges.</p></p>
