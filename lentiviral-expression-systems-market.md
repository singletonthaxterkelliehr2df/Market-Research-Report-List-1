<p><p>Lentiviral expression systems refer to a type of viral vector system derived from the human immunodeficiency virus (HIV). These systems are commonly employed in various genetic engineering applications, particularly in the transfer of genes into mammalian cells. Lentiviral vectors offer several advantages, such as their ability to infect both dividing and non-dividing cells, long-term stable gene expression, and large cargo capacity, making them a popular choice in gene therapy, vaccine development, and fundamental research.</p><p>The global lentiviral expression systems market is projected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Factors driving the market growth include the increasing prevalence of genetic disorders and chronic diseases, rising investments in research and development activities, and the growing demand for advanced gene therapy solutions. Additionally, technological advancements in lentiviral vector design and production processes are further fueling market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing adoption of lentiviral expression systems in drug discovery and development processes is also expected to contribute to market expansion. These systems enable the production of viral particles for virus-mediated cell transduction, which aids in studying specific gene functions and drug screening assays.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a growing focus on the development of safer and more efficient lentiviral systems. Researchers are exploring strategies to minimize the risk of genotoxicity associated with lentiviral vectors and improve their safety profiles.
