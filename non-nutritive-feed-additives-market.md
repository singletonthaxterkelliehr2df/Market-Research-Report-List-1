<p><p>Non-nutritive feed additives are substances added to animal feed in order to improve the overall health and performance of livestock. Some common non-nutritive feed additives include antibiotics, probiotics, enzymes, antioxidants, and acidifiers.</p><p>The non-nutritive feed additives market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for high-quality meat products, rising awareness about animal health, and the need to improve feed efficiency in livestock farming are driving the growth of this market. Additionally, the growing trend of organic farming and the ban on certain antibiotics in animal feed are also contributing to the growth of the non-nutritive feed additives market.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the non-nutritive feed additives market include the development of innovative additives such as prebiotics and essential oils, increasing research and development activities to improve the effectiveness of additives, and the adoption of sustainable farming practices.
