<p><p>Enameled copper wires are classified into various types based on the type of enamel coating used. These types include acetal enamelled wire, polyester enamelled wire, polyurethane enamelled wire, and composite coating enamelled wire. Acetal enamelled wire is known for its high thermal resistance, while polyester enamelled wire offers excellent solvent resistance. Polyurethane enamelled wire is preferred for its flexibility and abrasion resistance. Composite coating enamelled wire combines the properties of different enamel types, providing a versatile option. Other types of enamelled wire may include specialty coatings catering to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
