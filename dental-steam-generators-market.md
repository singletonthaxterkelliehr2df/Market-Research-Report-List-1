<p><p>Dental steam generators are used in dental clinics to produce steam for various dental equipment and procedures. There are two types of dental steam generators: integral boiler and remote boiler market. </p><p>Integral boiler steam generators have the boiler and steam generator integrated into one unit. This design allows for a compact and space-saving solution, as well as easy installation and maintenance. </p><p>Remote boiler steam generators, on the other hand, have a separate boiler unit that supplies steam to the steam generator. This type of system provides flexibility in terms of installation location and allows for a larger steam production capacity.</p></p>
