<p><p>The plastic zipper market can be categorized into three types: close-end zipper, open-end zipper, and two-way zipper. Close-end zippers are commonly used in items like pants and jackets, where the two ends of the zipper are closed together. Open-end zippers are commonly found in items like hoodies and bags, where the two ends can be completely separated. Two-way zippers have two sliders that allow the zipper to be opened from both ends, commonly used in items like coats and luggage.</p></p>
