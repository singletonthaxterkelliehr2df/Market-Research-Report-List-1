<p><p>NdFeB permanent magnets are classified into two main types based on their production methods: bonded NdFeB and sintered NdFeB. Bonded NdFeB magnets are made by mixing NdFeB powder with a polymer binder, resulting in lower magnetic properties but higher mechanical strength. Sintered NdFeB magnets, on the other hand, are produced through a powder metallurgy process, resulting in stronger magnetic properties but lower mechanical strength. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
