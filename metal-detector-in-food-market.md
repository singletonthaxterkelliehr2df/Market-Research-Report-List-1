<p><strong>The Metal Detector in Food Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Balanced Coil Systems</li><li>Ferrous-in-Foil Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal detectors in the food market come in two main types: Balanced Coil Systems and Ferrous-in-Foil Systems. Balanced Coil Systems are typically used for dry products like grains and powders, as they are highly sensitive to all types of metals. On the other hand, Ferrous-in-Foil Systems are specialized for detecting ferrous metals contained in foil packaging. Both types of metal detectors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and quality of food products by identifying any metal contaminants before they reach consumers.</p></p>
