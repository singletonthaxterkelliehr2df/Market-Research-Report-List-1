<p><p>Onion oil is obtained through different methods in the market: steam distillation, solvent extraction, and supercritical extraction. Steam distillation involves the use of steam to extract the oil from onion seeds. Solvent extraction involves using a solvent like hexane to separate the oil from the onion. Supercritical extraction involves using carbon dioxide under high pressure and temperature to extract the oil. Each method has its own advantages and is used based on the desired quality and purity of the final product.</p></p>
