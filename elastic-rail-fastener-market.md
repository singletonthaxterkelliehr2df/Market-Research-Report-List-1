<p><p>The elastic rail fastener market consists of various types of fasteners used in railway tracks. Concrete pillow fasteners are designed to support concrete sleepers, providing stability and reducing vibrations. Buckle plate fasteners secure steel plates to the sleepers, enhancing track stability and preventing rail movement. Spring fasteners use resilient springs to absorb shocks and vibrations, reducing track stress and enhancing safety. These fasteners play a crucial role in improving railway infrastructure and ensuring smooth and efficient train operations.</p></p>
