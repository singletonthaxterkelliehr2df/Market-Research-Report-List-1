<p><p>Electric control panels are categorized into different types based on their applications. Instrument control panels are used to monitor and control various processes in industries. Motor control panels are specifically designed to control the operation of motors. Lighting control panels are used for regulating and managing lighting systems. Generator control panels are used to monitor and control power generation from generators.</p></p>
