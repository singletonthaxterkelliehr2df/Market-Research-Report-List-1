<p><p>Data Centre Server market includes different types of servers designed to meet varying performance, space, and scalability requirements. Rack servers are compact and slide into standard server racks, blade servers are modular units that fit into a shared chassis, tower servers are standalone units resembling traditional desktop towers, microservers are small, energy-efficient servers ideal for low-intensity workloads, and Open Compute Project servers are customizable and open-source servers designed for maximum efficiency and cost savings. Each type offers distinct advantages suited to different data centre needs.</p></p>
