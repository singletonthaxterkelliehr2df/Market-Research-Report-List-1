<p><ul><li>Single Face Board</li><li>Single Wall Board</li><li>Double Wall Board</li><li>Triple Wall Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrugated board is available in various types to meet different packaging needs. Single face board consists of one layer of corrugated paper, while single wall board has one layer of corrugated paper sandwiched between two flat linerboards. Double wall board has two layers of corrugated paper and three flat linerboards, providing extra strength and durability. Triple wall board is the strongest type with three layers of corrugated paper and four flat linerboards. Each type caters to specific weight-bearing requirements and provides varying levels of protection for packaged goods.</p></p>
