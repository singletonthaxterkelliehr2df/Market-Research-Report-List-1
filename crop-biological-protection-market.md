<p><strong>The Crop Biological Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microbial Pesticides</li><li>Biochemical Pesticides</li><li>Plant-Incorporated Protectants (PIPs)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crop Biological Protection market types include Microbial Pesticides, which are naturally occurring bacteria, fungi, or viruses that target specific pests, Biochemical Pesticides, which are naturally derived substances like pheromones or plant extracts that disrupt pest behavior, and Plant-Incorporated Protectants (PIPs), which are genetically modified crops that produce their own pesticides. Other types of biological protection may involve natural predators or parasites of pests. These different methods provide environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional chemical pesticides in agricultural practices.</p></p>
