<p><p>RC servos are commonly classified into two main types: DC and AC servos. DC servos utilize direct current power sources and are generally more cost-effective and easier to control. They are commonly used in smaller, less demanding applications. On the other hand, AC servos are known for their superior performance and precision, making them suitable for high-end applications that require accurate positioning and control. They are typically more expensive but offer higher efficiency and reliability compared to DC servos.</p></p>
