<p><ul><li>P Type</li><li>N Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monocrystalline silicon ingots are classified based on the type of dopant used during the manufacturing process, which determines the conductivity of the material. P-type ingots have a positive electrical charge, while N-type ingots have a negative charge. P-type market refers to the segment of the industry that produces ingots with a majority of positive charge carriers, while N-type market focuses on ingots with a majority of negative charge carriers. Both types are essential for the production of various electronic devices such as solar cells and semiconductors.</p></p>
