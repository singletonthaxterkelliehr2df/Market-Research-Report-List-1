<p><p>Fresh beef packaging typically falls into different market types, including Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP), Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP), Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP), and others. MAP involves modifying the atmosphere inside the packaging to prolong the shelf life of the product. VSP uses a flexible film that adheres to the meat, providing a tight seal. VTP involves shaping a vacuum-sealed package around the meat. Other types of packaging may include specialized techniques or materials to ensure freshness and quality of the beef.</p></p>
