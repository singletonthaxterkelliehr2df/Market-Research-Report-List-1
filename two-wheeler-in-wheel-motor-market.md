<p><p>The Two-wheeler In-wheel Motor Market consists of two main types: Outer Rotor Type and Inner Rotor Type. Outer Rotor Type motors have the stator placed on the outer side of the rotor, resulting in a larger diameter and higher torque output. This type is suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Inner Rotor Type motors have the stator positioned inside the rotor, making them smaller in size and lighter in weight. These motors are typically used in lightweight and compact two-wheelers, such as electric bicycles and scooters.</p></p>
