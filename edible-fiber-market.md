<p><p>Edible fiber market can be divided into two main types: soluble fiber and insoluble fiber. Soluble fiber dissolves in water and forms a gel-like substance in the digestive tract, helping to lower cholesterol and regulate blood sugar levels. It can be found in fruits, vegetables, and oats. In contrast, insoluble fiber does not dissolve in water and adds bulk to the stool, aiding in digestion and preventing constipation. It is commonly found in whole grains and nuts. Both types of fiber are essential for a healthy diet.</p></p>
