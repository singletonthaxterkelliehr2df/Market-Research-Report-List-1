<p><ul><li>MS</li><li>HPS</li><li>EHPS</li><li>EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Steering-by-Wire System market consists of various types, including Mechanical Steering (MS), Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electric Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), and Electric Power Steering (EPS). MS utilizes mechanical linkage between the steering wheel and the wheels, while HPS uses hydraulic pressure to assist with steering. EHPS combines hydraulic and electric systems for improved performance, and EPS operates solely on electric power. These different types offer varying levels of efficiency, responsiveness, and control in automotive steering systems.</p></p>
