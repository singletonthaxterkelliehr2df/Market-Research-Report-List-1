<p><strong>The Polyethylene Pipe Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Coating</li><li>External Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene pipe coating market consists of two types - internal coating and external coating. Internal coating involves the application of a layer of polyethylene on the inner surface of pipes to protect against corrosion and improve flow efficiency. External coating, on the other hand, involves applying a layer of polyethylene on the outer surface of pipes to provide protection against environmental factors and mechanical damage. Both types of coatings are essential for extending the lifespan and ensuring the reliability of pipelines in various industries.</p></p>
