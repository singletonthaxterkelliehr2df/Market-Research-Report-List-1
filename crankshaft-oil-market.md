<p><p>The crankshaft oil market can be classified into three main types: liquid, semi-solid, and solid. Liquid crankshaft oil is the most commonly used type, as it easily flows and lubricates the moving parts of an engine. Semi-solid crankshaft oil is thicker in consistency and provides better protection against wear and friction. Solid crankshaft oil is rare and mainly used in specialized applications. Each type of crankshaft oil has its own benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the engine.</p></p>
