<p><strong>The Fiber-digested Silage Inoculants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homofermentative</li><li>Heterofermentative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber-digested silage inoculants are used to speed up the fermentation process in silage, improving its quality and nutrient content. There are two main types of inoculants: homofermentative and heterofermentative. </p><p>Homofermentative inoculants primarily produce lactic acid during fermentation, leading to a quick and efficient process. They are well-suited for high-moisture silages such as grasses.</p><p>Heterofermentative inoculants produce a mix of lactic acid, acetic acid, and other byproducts during fermentation. They are often used in lower-moisture silages like corn or alfalfa to prevent spoilage and improve aerobic stability.</p></p>
