<p><p>Form in Place (FIP) Gaskets are versatile sealing solutions used in various industries. There are two main types of FIP gaskets: Conductive and Non-Conductive. Conductive FIP gaskets are designed to provide electrical conductivity and are commonly used in electronic devices to prevent electromagnetic interference.
