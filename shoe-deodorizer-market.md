<p><p>The shoe deodorizer market can be categorized into two main types: powder shoe deodorizers and spray shoe deodorizers. Powder shoe deodorizers are typically in powder form and are applied directly inside the shoe to absorb moisture and eliminate odors. On the other hand, spray shoe deodorizers are in liquid form and can be sprayed directly onto the shoe to kill bacteria and neutralize odors. Both types of deodorizers are designed to keep shoes smelling fresh and clean, catering to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
