<p><strong>The Aircraft Towing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Towbar Tractors</li><li>Towbarless Tractors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft towing equipment market consists of two main types - towbar tractors and towbarless tractors. Towbar tractors are equipped with a towbar that attaches to the aircraft's nosewheel, providing a direct connection for towing. Towbarless tractors, on the other hand, use retractable lifting arms to securely lift and tow the entire aircraft without the need for a towbar. Both types of equipment are essential for safely moving aircraft within airports, with towbarless tractors offering more flexibility and efficiency in handling larger aircraft.</p></p>
