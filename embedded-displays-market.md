<p><p>Embedded displays market can be divided into different types based on the technology used. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) is a common type known for its low power consumption and high resolution. LED (Light Emitting Diode) displays offer bright and energy-efficient performance. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays provide vibrant colors and high contrast ratios.
