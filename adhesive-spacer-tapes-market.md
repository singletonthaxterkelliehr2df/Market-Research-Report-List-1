<p><p>Adhesive spacer tapes are available in two main types: double sided and single sided. Double sided spacer tapes have adhesive on both sides, allowing them to bond two surfaces together. These tapes are commonly used for mounting objects or joining materials. Single sided spacer tapes, on the other hand, have adhesive on only one side and are typically used for insulation or cushioning purposes. Both types of tapes offer strong adhesion and versatility in various applications.</p></p>
