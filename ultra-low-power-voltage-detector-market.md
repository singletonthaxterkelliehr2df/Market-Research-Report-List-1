P-ch detectors utilize P-channel MOSFETs to achieve low power consumption. CMOS detectors combine both N-ch and P-ch transistors to offer high performance and efficiency. Each type has its own advantages and applications in different scenarios, catering to the diverse needs of various industries requiring ultra low power voltage detection solutions.</p></p>
