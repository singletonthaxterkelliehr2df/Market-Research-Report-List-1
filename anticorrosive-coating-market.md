<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne Coating</li><li>Water-borne Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anticorrosive coating market is divided into two main types: solvent-borne coating and water-borne coating. Solvent-borne coatings are oil-based and contain volatile organic compounds, making them more durable and resistant to harsh environments. On the other hand, water-borne coatings are environmentally friendly as they have lower VOC levels and are easier to clean up. Both types offer protection against corrosion and are used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
