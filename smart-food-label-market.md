<p><strong>The Smart Food Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RFID</li><li>Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Food Label market includes two main types: RFID and Sensing technology. RFID technology uses radio waves to automatically identify and track food products throughout the supply chain, allowing for efficient inventory management and enhanced food safety. Sensing technology, on the other hand, involves the use of sensors to monitor various aspects of food quality, such as temperature and freshness, providing real-time information to both consumers and producers. Both types of technology aim to enhance traceability, transparency, and overall efficiency in the food industry.</p></p>
