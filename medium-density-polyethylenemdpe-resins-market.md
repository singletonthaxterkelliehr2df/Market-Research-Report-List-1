<p><p>Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) Resins Market includes two major types of production processes, Autoclave Process and Tubular Process. Autoclave process involves polymerization of ethylene at high pressure and temperature in presence of a catalyst in a stirred reactor, whereas Tubular process involves polymerization of ethylene in a tubular reactor at high pressure and temperature. Both processes result in the production of MDPE resins with varying properties and applications, catering to different market segments.</p></p>
