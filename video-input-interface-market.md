<p><ul><li>Mini-DIN</li><li>D-subminiature</li><li>Vivo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video input interfaces are crucial for connecting external video sources to monitors or display devices. Common market types include Mini-DIN, D-subminiature, and Vivo. Mini-DIN interfaces, often seen in older devices, feature small circular connectors. D-subminiature interfaces are commonly used in VGA connections, with a characteristic D-shaped plug. Vivo interfaces, on the other hand, offer both video and audio capabilities through a single cable. Each market type caters to specific needs and preferences, providing users with a variety of options for connecting their devices.</p></p>
