<p><p>Metal Oxide Varistors (MOV) are categorized into two main types - SMD Type and Leaded Type. SMD Type MOVs, also known as surface mount devices, are designed for compact and high-density circuit boards. They are easy to install and provide efficient protection against voltage surges. On the other hand, Leaded Type MOVs have wire leads that make them suitable for through-hole mounting in electronic devices. Both types of MOVs are essential components in various electronic applications to safeguard equipment from overvoltage conditions.</p></p>
