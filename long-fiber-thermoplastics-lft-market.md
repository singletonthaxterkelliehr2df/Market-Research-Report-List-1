<p><p>Long Fiber Thermoplastics (LFT) are high-performance materials known for their strength, stiffness, and impact resistance. In the market, there are two main types of LFTs: LFT-G and LFT-D. LFT-G, or LFT-Glass, is reinforced with glass fibers to enhance mechanical properties such as strength and stiffness. On the other hand, LFT-D, or LFT-Direct, is reinforced with other materials like carbon or natural fibers for specific applications that require different characteristics. Both types of LFTs offer improved performance compared to traditional thermoplastics in various industries.</p></p>
