<p><p>Zinc oxide powder is produced through three main processes: direct, indirect, and wet chemical. Direct process involves the vaporization of zinc metal, while indirect process utilizes zinc ore as the starting material. Wet chemical process involves the precipitation of zinc carbonate from a zinc salt solution. Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of cost, purity, and environmental impact. The choice of process depends on the specific requirements of the end product and market demand.</p></p>
