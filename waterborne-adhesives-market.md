<p><ul><li>Starch/Dextrin Adhesives</li><li>Protein/Casein Adhesives</li><li>Vinyl Acetate Adhesives</li><li>Rubber Latex Adhesives</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterborne adhesives are categorized into different types based on the materials used in their composition. Starch/Dextrin adhesives are derived from natural sources like corn or wheat. Protein/Casein adhesives are made from animal proteins. Vinyl Acetate adhesives are synthetic polymers that offer good adhesion properties. Rubber Latex adhesives are derived from natural rubber sources. Other types of waterborne adhesives include polyurethane and acrylic adhesives. Each type of adhesive offers unique properties and is suited for different applications in industries such as packaging, construction, and woodworking.</p></p>
