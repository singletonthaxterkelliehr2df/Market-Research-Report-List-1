<p><p>Connected thermostats come in two main types: wired and wireless. Wired connected thermostats are physically connected to the heating or cooling system in a building through wires, allowing for precise control and monitoring. On the other hand, wireless connected thermostats use either Wi-Fi or Bluetooth technology to remotely control the temperature settings from a smartphone or other smart devices. Both types offer convenience, energy efficiency, and the ability to adjust settings remotely for optimal comfort and cost savings.</p></p>
