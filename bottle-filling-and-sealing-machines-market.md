<p><p>Fully-automatic bottle filling and sealing machines require minimal human intervention as they can complete the entire process from filling to sealing automatically. These machines are generally used in large-scale production facilities due to their high efficiency and productivity. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention during the filling and sealing process. They are ideal for small to medium scale production operations where the production volume is not as high as in large-scale facilities. Both types of machines play a crucial role in streamlining the bottling process for various industries.</p></p>
