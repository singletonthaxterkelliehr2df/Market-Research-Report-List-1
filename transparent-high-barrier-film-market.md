<p><p>Transparent high barrier films are commonly categorized into different types such as PET (polyethylene terephthalate), CPP (cast polypropylene), BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene), PVA (polyvinyl alcohol), PLA (polylactic acid), and others. PET films are known for their excellent barrier properties against moisture and gases. CPP films are flexible and have good heat-sealing properties. BOPP films offer high stiffness and clarity. PVA films are biodegradable and have good oxygen barrier properties. PLA films are also biodegradable and have good moisture barrier properties. Other types of high barrier films include PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) and EVOH (ethylene vinyl alcohol).</p></p>
