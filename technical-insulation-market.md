<p><p>Technical insulation market can be categorized into three main types: flexible foam, rigid foam, and man-made mineral fiber. Flexible foam insulation is versatile and easy to install, making it ideal for a variety of applications. Rigid foam insulation provides excellent thermal performance and is often used in construction projects. Man-made mineral fiber insulation, such as fiberglass and rock wool, is known for its fire resistance and sound absorption properties. Each type of insulation offers different benefits and is suitable for various projects depending on specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
