<p><p>Intensive Care Neonatal Ventilators market includes two main types based on their mode of delivery: Invasive Ventilators and Non-Invasive Ventilators. Invasive Ventilators are used when the patient's airway needs mechanical support through an endotracheal tube, while Non-Invasive Ventilators provide respiratory support without the need for an artificial airway. Both types are essential in providing life-saving care to newborns in critical condition, with Invasive Ventilators being commonly used in more severe cases, while Non-Invasive Ventilators are preferred for less severe cases.</p></p>
