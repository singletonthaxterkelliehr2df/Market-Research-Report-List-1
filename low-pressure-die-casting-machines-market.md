<p><p>Low-pressure die casting machines are available in two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical machines are ideal for casting complex shapes and thin-walled components, as they allow for gravity-assisted casting. On the other hand, horizontal machines are commonly used for producing larger and heavier components due to their ability to accommodate larger molds. Both types offer their own set of advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the casting process.</p></p>
