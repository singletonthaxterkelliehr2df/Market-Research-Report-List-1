<p><ul><li>Hardline Cable</li><li>Triaxial Cable</li><li>Twin Axial Cable</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro coaxial cables are used in various industries and come in different types. Hardline cables are thick, rigid cables used for high-frequency applications. Triaxial cables have an additional outer conductor for improved signal quality. Twin axial cables have two inner conductors twisted together for lower crosstalk. Other types of micro coaxial cables include semi-rigid cables, flexible cables, and ultra-flexible cables. Each type has its own specific use and benefits, catering to different requirements in the market.</p></p>
