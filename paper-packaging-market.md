<p><p>Paper packaging market can be categorized into three main types - Paperboard, Container Board, and Corrugated Board. Paperboard is a thicker and more durable type of paper used for packaging luxury goods and consumer products. Container Board is a sturdy type of paper used for manufacturing shipping boxes and cartons. Corrugated Board is made up of multiple layers of paper with a fluted layer in between, making it strong and ideal for shipping fragile items. These three types cater to different packaging needs and industries.</p></p>
