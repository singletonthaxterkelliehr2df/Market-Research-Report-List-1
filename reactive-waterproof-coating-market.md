<p><p>Reactive Waterproof Coating market is divided into two types: Liquid Reactive Waterproof Coating and Dry Reactive Waterproof Coating. The Liquid Reactive Waterproof Coating is applied in liquid form and forms a protective layer upon drying, providing resistance against water damage. On the other hand, the Dry Reactive Waterproof Coating is a dry material that is mixed with water to create a paste or slurry. It is then applied to the surface, where it hardens to form a protective barrier against water penetration. Both types offer effective waterproofing solutions for various applications.</p></p>
