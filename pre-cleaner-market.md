<p><p>The Pre-Cleaner market is segmented into different types such as Drum Pre-Cleaner and Vibrating Mesh Pre-Cleaner. Drum Pre-Cleaners are designed with rotating drums that separate impurities from grains through a series of screens. On the other hand, Vibrating Mesh Pre-Cleaners use vibrating screens to separate unwanted materials from grains. Both types of Pre-Cleaners are essential in the agricultural industry to improve the quality of grains and seeds before further processing.</p></p>
