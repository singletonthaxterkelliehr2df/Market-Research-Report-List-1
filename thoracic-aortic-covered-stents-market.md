<p><p>Thoracic Aortic Covered Stents are medical devices used to treat aortic aneurysms. There are two main types in the market: Bifurcated Type and Straight Tube Type. Bifurcated Type stents have branches that extend into two separate blood vessels, allowing for blood flow to be maintained. Straight Tube Type stents, on the other hand, have a single tube design that extends along the length of the aorta. Both types of stents serve the purpose of reinforcing the weakened aortic wall and preventing potential ruptures.</p></p>
