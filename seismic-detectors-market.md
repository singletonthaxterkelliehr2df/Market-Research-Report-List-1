<p><p>Normal Seismic Detectors are traditional devices that detect ground vibrations caused by seismic activity. They provide basic information on the intensity and location of the tremors. On the other hand, Intelligent Seismic Detectors are advanced systems that not only detect seismic activity but also analyze data in real-time to predict the impact and potential damage.
