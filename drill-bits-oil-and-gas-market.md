<p><p>Drill Bits (Oil and Gas) Market consists of two main types: Fixed Cutter Bits and Roller Cone Bits. Fixed Cutter Bits have diamond or tungsten carbide inserts that shear rock formation as it rotates, making them ideal for hard and abrasive formations. Roller Cone Bits, on the other hand, have rotating cones with teeth that crush and chip away at the rock formations. Both types are essential in the oil and gas industry for drilling wells efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
