<p><strong>The Advanced Power Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs)</li><li>Power Integrated Modules (PIMs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) are advanced power modules that integrate power devices, control circuits, and protection functions in a single package. They offer enhanced efficiency, reliability, and performance in various applications, such as motor drives, inverters, and power supplies. On the other hand, Power Integrated Modules (PIMs) are another type of advanced power modules that combine power devices and control circuits. They are commonly used in high-power applications like industrial drives and renewable energy systems. Both IPMs and PIMs contribute to simplifying designs and improving overall system efficiency.</p></p>
