<p><strong>The Bonded Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Surface Coatings</li><li>External Surface Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bonded coatings can be classified into two main types: internal surface coatings and external surface coatings. Internal surface coatings are applied to the inner surfaces of containers, pipes, and other products to protect against corrosion and improve durability. External surface coatings are applied to the outer surfaces of products to provide protection against environmental factors, such as weathering, UV exposure, and abrasion. Both types of coatings play a crucial role in preserving the integrity and lifespan of various products.</p></p>
