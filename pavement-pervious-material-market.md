<p><p>Pavement pervious materials refer to surfaces that allow water to pass through them. The market includes different types such as permeable grid systems, porous concrete, porous asphalt, and other innovative materials. Permeable grid systems consist of interlocking pavers with open joints that allow water to infiltrate the ground. Porous concrete is a mixture of cement, water, and coarse aggregate that forms a porous surface. Porous asphalt is made of bitumen and aggregates, allowing water to drain through it. Other materials include sustainable alternatives for permeable pavements.</p></p>
