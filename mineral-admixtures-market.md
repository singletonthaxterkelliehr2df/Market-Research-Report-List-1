<p><p>Mineral admixtures in the construction industry are materials added to concrete to enhance its properties. Cementitious materials, a type of mineral admixture, include materials like fly ash and slag that react with the calcium hydroxide in cement to form additional cementitious compounds. Pozzolans are another type of mineral admixture that react with calcium hydroxide to form cementitious compounds, improving the strength and durability of concrete. Both cementitious materials and pozzolans are crucial in enhancing the performance of concrete in various construction applications.</p></p>
