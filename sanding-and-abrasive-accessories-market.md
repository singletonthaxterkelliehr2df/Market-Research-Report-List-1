<p><p>Sanding and abrasive accessories market can be categorized into three main types - bonded, coated, and others. Bonded abrasives are made by bonding abrasive particles together into a wheel or a grinding disc. Coated abrasives have abrasive particles bonded to a flexible backing material like paper or cloth. The "others" category includes loose abrasives, superabrasives, and specialty abrasives. Each type of market offers a variety of products suited for different applications and industries, providing options for various sanding and grinding needs.</p></p>
