<p><p>Polymeric film for separation comes in various types including hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes are made up of dense polymeric material in a tubular form, offering high surface area for separation. Spiral wound membranes consist of flat sheets wound around a central tube, providing a compact design suitable for high-pressure applications. Other types of polymeric film for separation include tubular, capillary, and ceramic membranes, offering different properties and performance characteristics for various separation processes.</p></p>
