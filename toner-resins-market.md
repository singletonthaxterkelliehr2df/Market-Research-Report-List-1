<p><strong>The Toner Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic</li><li>Non-Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Toner resins are used in the production of toner cartridges for printers. The market is divided into magnetic and non-magnetic types. Magnetic toner resins contain iron oxide particles, which are attracted to the magnetic drum in the printer. This type is commonly used in laser printers. Non-magnetic toner resins do not contain iron oxide and are used in inkjet printers. Both types are essential for producing high-quality prints and play a crucial role in the printing industry.</p></p>
