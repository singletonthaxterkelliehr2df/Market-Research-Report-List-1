<p><ul><li>Intraoral Film Processors</li><li>Extraoral Film Processors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental film processors are categorized into two main types: intraoral film processors and extraoral film processors. Intraoral film processors are used to develop X-ray films taken inside the mouth, providing detailed images of teeth and surrounding tissues. Extraoral film processors, on the other hand, are designed to develop X-ray films taken outside the mouth, such as panoramic or cephalometric images. These different types of film processors cater to specific dental imaging needs, ensuring high-quality and accurate diagnostic results for dental professionals.</p></p>
