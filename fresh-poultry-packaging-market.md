<p><p>Fresh poultry packaging market offers various types of packaging solutions to ensure the safety and prolong the shelf life of the products. Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) involves modifying the atmosphere inside the package to slow down spoilage. Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) tightly seals the product in a film to prevent oxygen exposure. Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP) uses heat to form a tight seal around the product. Other types of packaging options include active packaging, intelligent packaging, and sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
