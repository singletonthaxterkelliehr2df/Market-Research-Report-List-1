<p><p>Sedimentation and centrifugation equipment market types include clarifiers, disk centrifuges, decanters, dissolved air flotation systems, hydrocyclones, and other equipment. Clarifiers are used to separate solids from liquids in wastewater treatment processes. Disk centrifuges are used for separating and clarifying liquids with high-speed rotating disks. Decanters are used for continuous separation of solids from liquids in industrial processes. Dissolved air flotation systems remove suspended solids from wastewater by introducing fine bubbles. Hydrocyclones are used for liquid-liquid separation or solid-liquid classification in various industries. Other equipment includes different types of centrifuges and sedimentation tanks used for similar applications.</p></p>
