<p><p>Skin ulcers negative pressure therapy market is segmented into four main types: pressure ulcers, diabetic ulcers, venous ulcers, and arterial ulcers. Pressure ulcers develop from prolonged pressure on the skin, commonly seen in bedridden patients. Diabetic ulcers occur in diabetic patients due to poor circulation and nerve damage. Venous ulcers result from poor blood circulation in the legs, often seen in individuals with venous insufficiency. Arterial ulcers develop from restricted blood flow to the limbs, commonly seen in patients with peripheral artery disease.</p></p>
