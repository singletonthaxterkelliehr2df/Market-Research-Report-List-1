<p><p>Straight-cut gears are commonly categorized into three main types based on their design and shape: Cylindrical, Umbrella, and Others. Cylindrical gears have straight and parallel teeth cut along the cylinder's surface. Umbrella gears have teeth cut at an angle to the gear axis, resembling an umbrella when viewed from above. The Others category includes various unique designs and shapes that do not fit into the traditional cylindrical or umbrella gear types. Each type has its own advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
