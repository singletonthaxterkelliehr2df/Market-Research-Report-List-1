Rollstock is a continuous roll of plastic film used in automated packaging machines. Gusseted bags have expandable sides for bulkier items. Wicketed bags are bundles of bags attached to a wire for easy dispensing. Wraps are used for wrapping products like candy or sandwiches.</p></p>
