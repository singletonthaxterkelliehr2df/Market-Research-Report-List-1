<p><strong>The Field Installable Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Fusion Splice Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Field Installable Connector Market Types include Mechanical Type and Fusion Splice Type. Mechanical Type connectors are pre-polished and require no epoxy or polishing, making them quick and easy to install in the field. They are ideal for temporary or low-budget installations. Fusion Splice Type connectors involve a complex fusion process that creates a permanent, low-loss connection. They are typically used for high-speed, high-bandwidth applications where signal loss must be minimized. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the installation.</p></p>
