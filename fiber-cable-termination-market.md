<p><strong>The Fiber Cable Termination Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber Optic Pigtails</li><li>Fanout Kits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber cable termination market includes various types of products, such as fiber optic pigtails and fanout kits. Fiber optic pigtails are short sections of fiber cables that are terminated with connectors at one end and left bare at the other end. They are used to connect optical fibers to devices, such as patch panels or network equipment. On the other hand, fanout kits are bundles of tight-buffered fibers that are separated into individual fibers for termination. These kits simplify the process of terminating multiple fibers and ensure efficient cable management.</p></p>
