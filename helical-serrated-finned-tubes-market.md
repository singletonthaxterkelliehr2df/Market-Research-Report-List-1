<p><p>Helical serrated finned tubes are used in various industries for heat transfer applications. There are two main types in the market: single metal finned tubes and bimetal composite finned tubes. Single metal finned tubes consist of a single metal material for both the tube and the fin, providing efficient heat transfer. On the other hand, bimetal composite finned tubes have a tube made of one metal and fins made of another metal, offering enhanced corrosion resistance and thermal conductivity. Both types cater to different industry requirements for optimal heat transfer solutions.</p></p>
