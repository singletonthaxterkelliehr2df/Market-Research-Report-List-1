<p><p>A rotary evaporator, also known as a rotovap, is a laboratory tool used for the efficient and gentle removal of solvents from samples by evaporation. It consists of a heating bath, rotating flask, condenser, and a motor that rotates the flask, allowing for efficient evaporation of the solvent at a reduced pressure. Rotary evaporators are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemical laboratories, food and beverage, environmental analysis, and academia.</p><p>The rotary evaporator market is experiencing steady growth and is expected to expand at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period.
