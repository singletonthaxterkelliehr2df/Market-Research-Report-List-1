<p><p>The ceramic catalyst substrate market can be segmented into two main types based on the material used: cordierite material and silicon carbide material. Cordierite material is known for its high thermal shock resistance and low thermal expansion, making it ideal for use in automotive catalytic converters. On the other hand, silicon carbide material offers excellent mechanical strength and thermal conductivity, making it suitable for high temperature applications. Both materials play a crucial role in reducing harmful emissions and improving air quality in various industries.</p></p>
