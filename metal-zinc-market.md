<p><p>The metal zinc market can be categorized into two main types: zinc and zinc alloys. Zinc is primarily used for galvanizing steel to prevent corrosion, as well as in the production of batteries and various chemical compounds. Zinc alloys, on the other hand, are mixtures of zinc with other metals such as copper, aluminum, and magnesium to enhance strength, durability, and resistance to corrosion. These alloys are commonly used in automotive, construction, and electronics industries.</p></p>
