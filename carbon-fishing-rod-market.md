<p><p>The carbon fishing rod market is divided into two main types based on the type of water they are designed for: saltwater fishing rods and freshwater rods. Saltwater fishing rods are specifically crafted to withstand the corrosive nature of saltwater environments and tackle larger, more powerful fish species. Freshwater rods, on the other hand, are designed for use in lakes, rivers, and streams, and are usually lighter in weight and more sensitive to the movements of smaller fish species found in freshwater environments.</p></p>
