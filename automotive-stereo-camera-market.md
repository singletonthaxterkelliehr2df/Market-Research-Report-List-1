<p><ul><li>Dynamic Stereo Camera</li><li>Static Stereo Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dynamic Stereo Camera in the automotive stereo camera market refers to cameras that are able to adjust their focus and capture images in real-time as the vehicle is in motion. These cameras provide a continuous stream of data for applications such as lane departure warning systems and pedestrian detection. On the other hand, Static Stereo Cameras are fixed cameras that capture images from a stationary position, typically used for functions such as parking assistance and 360-degree view systems. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing safety and convenience in vehicles.</p></p>
