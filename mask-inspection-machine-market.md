<p><strong>The Mask Inspection Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Inspection Machine Market includes the Die to Die (DD) Method and Die to Database (DB) Method. In the Die to Die method, the machine compares two masks to detect defects by aligning the images of the same layers. On the other hand, the Die to Database method compares the mask data with a reference database to identify any discrepancies. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and accuracy of masks used in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
