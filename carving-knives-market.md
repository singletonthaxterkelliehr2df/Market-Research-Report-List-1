<p><p>Carving knives come in different types based on the blade design: straight and curved. Straight carving knives have a long, straight blade that is ideal for making precise cuts and slicing through large pieces of meat or vegetables. On the other hand, curved carving knives have a blade that is slightly curved, which allows for rocking motion while cutting and is more suitable for removing meat from bones or slicing roasts. Both types of carving knives have their own unique advantages and are used for various culinary tasks.</p></p>
