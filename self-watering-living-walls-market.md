<p><ul><li>Soil-based</li><li>Water-based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-watering living walls come in three main types of market- soil-based, water-based, and others. Soil-based systems rely on a soil reservoir to hold water and deliver it to plants through capillary action. Water-based systems use a reservoir of water that is pumped or dripped onto the plants as needed. Other systems may use a combination of soil and water or different methods altogether to keep plants hydrated. Each type offers unique advantages and considerations for maintaining healthy green walls.</p></p>
