<p><p>Total Airport Management System (TAMS) encompasses various market types to ensure efficient airport operations. The Air Traffic Control Administrator System (ATCAS) manages air traffic control operations, while the Over Flight Billing System (ATCBILL) handles billing for overflights. The Real Time Apron Management System (RAMP) coordinates apron activities, and the Handling Agent System (HAS) streamlines ground handling processes. The Airport Flight Information Display System (AFIDS 2000) displays flight information, Gate Allocation System manages gate assignments, and the Automated Warehouse System oversees airport logistics. Other markets may include security systems, passenger processing solutions, and airport facility management technology.</p></p>
