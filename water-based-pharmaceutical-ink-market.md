<p><p>Water-based pharmaceutical ink can be categorized into three main types based on their physical state: solids, semi-solids, and liquids. Solid pharmaceutical inks are in the form of powders or granules, while semi-solids are pastes or gels with a thicker consistency. Liquid pharmaceutical inks are in a fluid state and are most commonly used for coating or printing on tablets and capsules. These different types cater to various requirements in the pharmaceutical industry, providing options for different applications and formulations.</p></p>
