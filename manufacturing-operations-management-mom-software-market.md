<p><p>Manufacturing Operations Management (MOM) software refers to the use of technology solutions to optimize and streamline manufacturing processes. The market for MOM software can be classified into two types: MES/MOM and MOM. MES/MOM (Manufacturing Execution System/Manufacturing Operations Management) focuses on real-time data collection, analysis, and decision-making to improve efficiency. It integrates various systems and provides a centralized view of production. MOM, on the other hand, encompasses a broader range of functionalities, including process control, quality management, maintenance, and more. Both types aim to enhance productivity, reduce downtime, ensure quality, and drive continuous improvement in manufacturing operations.</p></p>
