<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Type</li><li>Cooled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal imaging technology is categorized into two main types based on the type of detector used: uncooled and cooled. Uncooled thermal imaging systems do not require cooling mechanisms, making them smaller, more cost-effective, and easier to operate. They are commonly used in consumer applications such as night vision cameras and firefighting equipment. On the other hand, cooled thermal imaging systems have higher sensitivity and resolution, making them ideal for military, defense, and industrial applications where precision and long-range detection are crucial.</p></p>
