<p><p>Galvanic isolated gate drivers are used in applications where electrical isolation is essential to protect sensitive control circuits from high voltages. Isolated IGBT gate drivers are designed specifically for driving insulated gate bipolar transistors, while isolated MOSFET gate drivers are intended for driving metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors. Both types of gate drivers provide isolation between the input and output circuits to prevent electrical faults and ensure safe operation in a variety of industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
