<p><p>Low profile electrolytic copper foil is classified into three main types based on their thickness: below 15 μm, 15 μm-40 μm, and above 40 μm. Below 15 μm copper foil is used for applications requiring ultra-thin materials, such as flexible printed circuits. The 15 μm-40 μm thickness range is commonly used in high-speed digital signal transmission applications. Above 40 μm copper foil is typically used in power electronics and high current capacity applications. Each type offers specific benefits and is chosen based on the requirements of the application.</p></p>
