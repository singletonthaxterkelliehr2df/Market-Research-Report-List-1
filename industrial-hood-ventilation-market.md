<p><p>Industrial hood ventilation market offers various types of hoods to suit different needs. Closed exhaust hood is sealed to prevent contaminants from escaping. Cabinet exhaust hood is mounted over equipment to capture fumes. External exhaust hood directs air outside the building. Receiving exhaust hood captures emissions from equipment. Grooved exhaust hood enhances air capture efficiency. Blow suction hood uses fans to draw in air. Other types include custom-designed hoods to meet specific requirements in industrial settings. Each type of hood serves a unique purpose in maintaining air quality and safety in industrial environments.</p></p>
