<p><ul><li>Hybrid SiC Modules</li><li>Full SiC Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SiC power modules come in two main types: hybrid SiC modules and full SiC modules. Hybrid SiC modules utilize both SiC and traditional silicon materials, offering a balance between cost and performance. On the other hand, full SiC modules exclusively use SiC materials, providing higher efficiency and power density. Hybrid SiC modules are more cost-effective, while full SiC modules offer superior performance. Both types cater to different market needs, with hybrid modules being ideal for cost-sensitive applications and full modules for high-performance requirements.</p></p>
