<p><p>The passenger car lighting market offers different types of lighting options, including xenon lights, halogen lights, and LED lights. Xenon lights use xenon gas to produce a bright white light, providing better visibility and a stylish appearance. Halogen lights use a tungsten filament enclosed in a halogen gas-filled bulb, emitting a yellowish light. LED (Light Emitting Diode) lights use a semiconductor to emit light, offering energy efficiency, long lifespan, and various color options. These lighting options cater to the diverse preferences and requirements of car owners in terms of brightness, style, energy efficiency, and durability.</p></p>
