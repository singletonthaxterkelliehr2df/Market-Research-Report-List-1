<p><p>Medical surgical suction pumps are devices used to remove fluids, such as blood or mucus, from a patient's body during surgery. They come in three main types: electric, manual, and pneumatic. Electric suction pumps are powered by electricity and are commonly used in hospitals and clinics. Manual suction pumps require the user to manually operate the device to create suction. Pneumatic suction pumps utilize compressed gas to create suction. Each type of suction pump serves a specific purpose and is chosen based on the clinical needs and preferences of the healthcare provider.</p></p>
