<p><p>Nylon membrane filters are categorized based on their pore sizes into three main markets: below 0.45 µm pore sizes, 0.45 to 6 µm pore sizes, and above 6 µm pore sizes. Filters with below 0.45 µm pore sizes are primarily used for fine particle filtration, while filters with pore sizes between 0.45 to 6 µm are ideal for general filtration applications. Filters with above 6 µm pore sizes are commonly used for larger particle filtration and clarification purposes in various industries.</p></p>
