<p><strong>The LED Protectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Protectors</li><li>Surge Protectors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED protectors market can be categorized into three main types: shunt protectors, surge protectors, and others. Shunt protectors are designed to divert excess current away from the LED to prevent damage. Surge protectors are used to protect the LED from sudden spikes in voltage. The "others" category may include various types of protectors such as overvoltage protectors, thermal protectors, and current-limiting protectors. Each type of protector plays a crucial role in safeguarding LEDs from electrical issues and ensuring their longevity and performance.</p></p>
