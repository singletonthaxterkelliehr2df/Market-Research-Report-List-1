<p><p>Mining equipment tires can be categorized into three main types: Pneumatic, Solid, and Polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and offer good shock absorption and traction, making them suitable for rough terrains. Solid tires are made of solid rubber and provide resistance to punctures and maintenance-free operation. Polyurethane tires are lightweight and durable, offering excellent resistance to chemicals and abrasion. Each type of tire has its own unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the mining operation.</p></p>
