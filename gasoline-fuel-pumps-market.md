<p><ul><li>Mechanical Fuel Pump</li><li>Electric Fuel Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gasoline fuel pumps can be categorized into two main types: mechanical fuel pumps and electric fuel pumps. Mechanical fuel pumps are driven by the engine camshaft and use a diaphragm or piston to create suction, while electric fuel pumps are powered by the vehicle's electrical system and use a motor to transfer fuel from the tank to the engine. Both types of fuel pumps are essential components in delivering gasoline to the engine for combustion in vehicles.</p></p>
