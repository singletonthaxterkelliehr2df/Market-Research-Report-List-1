<p><p>The minimally invasive surgery retractors market includes various types such as 2-way, 3-way, and 4-way retractors. These retractors are used to hold and separate the surgical incision during minimally invasive surgeries. 2-way retractors enable separation in two directions, while 3-way retractors allow separation in three directions. Similarly, 4-way retractors provide separation in four directions. These different types of retractors offer surgeons flexibility in accessing the surgical site, facilitating the successful completion of minimally invasive procedures with minimal tissue damage and faster patient recovery.</p></p>
