<p><p>Asphalt shingles roofing materials are available in two main types: organic materials and fiberglass. Organic materials are composed of a base mat made from recycled paper saturated with asphalt for water and fire resistance. Fiberglass shingles, on the other hand, have a fiberglass mat coated with asphalt for durability and strength. Both types of materials are popular choices for roofing due to their affordability, ease of installation, and variety of styles and colors available in the market.</p></p>
