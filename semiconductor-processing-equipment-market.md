<p><p>Semiconductor processing equipment consists of various types such as lithography, wafer surface conditioning, and cleaning processes. Lithography equipment is used for transferring patterns onto the semiconductor wafer. Wafer surface conditioning equipment helps in preparing the wafer surface for further processing steps, such as deposition and etching. Cleaning processes equipment is crucial for maintaining the cleanliness of the wafers to ensure the quality and accuracy of the semiconductor devices being produced. These types of equipment are essential for the efficient manufacturing of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
