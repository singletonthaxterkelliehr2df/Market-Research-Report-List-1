<p><p>Automotive glass for windscreens is typically available in three main types: tempered, laminated, and other options. Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and safety, shattering into small, blunt pieces upon impact. Laminated glass consists of a layer of polyvinyl butyral between two sheets of glass, providing improved resistance to breakage and enhanced sound insulation. Other options may include acoustic glass or solar control glass, offering additional benefits such as noise reduction or UV protection. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the automotive market.</p></p>
