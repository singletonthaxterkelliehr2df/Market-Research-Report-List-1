<p><ul><li>Liquid Hardening Accelerator</li><li>Dry Powder Hardening Accelerator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hardening accelerators are commonly categorized into two types: liquid hardening accelerators and dry powder hardening accelerators. Liquid hardening accelerators are typically applied by spraying or adding directly to the concrete mix before pouring, while dry powder hardening accelerators are mixed in with the other dry ingredients before adding water. Both types of accelerators work by speeding up the hydration process of the concrete, resulting in faster setting times and increased early strength development. Each type offers unique benefits and application methods depending on the specific project requirements.</p></p>
