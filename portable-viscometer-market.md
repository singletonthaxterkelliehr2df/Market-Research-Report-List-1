<p><strong>The Portable Viscometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Type</li><li>Rotary Type</li><li>Vibrating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable viscometer market can be categorized into three main types: tube type, rotary type, and vibrating type. Tube type viscometers measure viscosity by timing the flow of liquid through a capillary tube. Rotary type viscometers rotate a spindle in the liquid to measure resistance to rotation. Vibrating type viscometers measure viscosity by analyzing the damping effect of a vibrating probe in the liquid. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications based on viscosity measurement requirements.</p></p>
