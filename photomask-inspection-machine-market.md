<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Inspection Machine market comprises two main types based on the inspection methods - Die to Die (DD) Method and Die to Database (DB) Method. In the Die to Die (DD) Method, the photomask is inspected by comparing die structures on the mask itself. In contrast, the Die to Database (DB) Method involves comparing the die structures on the mask to a digital database of the intended design. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring the accuracy and quality of photomasks used in semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
