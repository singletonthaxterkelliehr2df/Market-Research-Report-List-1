<p><p>Juice extractors come in different types catering to various consumer needs. Centrifugal juicers are popular for their high-speed spinning blades that quickly extract juice from fruits and vegetables. Masticating juicers, on the other hand, use a slow grinding and chewing motion to extract juice, preserving more nutrients and enzymes. Other market types may include citrus juicers, manual juicers, or hybrid models combining features of both centrifugal and masticating juicers. Each type offers distinct advantages and may suit different preferences or needs.</p></p>
