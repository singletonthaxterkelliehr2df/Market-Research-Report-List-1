<p><p>Plastic 3D printing powder can be classified into two types based on their reusability: reusable and not reusable. Reusable powders can be used multiple times in the printing process, allowing for cost savings and reduced waste. On the other hand, not reusable powders are single-use and must be discarded after each printing job, leading to higher material costs and increased waste generation. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, depending on the specific needs and priorities of the user.</p></p>
