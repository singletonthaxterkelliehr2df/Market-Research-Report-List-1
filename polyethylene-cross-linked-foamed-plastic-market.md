<p><ul><li>Chemical Crosslinking</li><li>Electronic Crosslinking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene Cross-Linked Foamed Plastic Market consists of two main types - Chemical Crosslinking and Electronic Crosslinking. Chemical Crosslinking involves the use of chemical agents to form strong bonds between polymer chains, resulting in a durable and heat-resistant foam. On the other hand, Electronic Crosslinking utilizes high-energy radiation to create crosslinks within the polyethylene molecules, yielding a lightweight and soft foam material. Both types cater to various industries such as packaging, construction, automotive, and electronics due to their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
