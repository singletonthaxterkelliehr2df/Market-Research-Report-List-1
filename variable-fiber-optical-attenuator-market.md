<p><p>Variable Fiber Optical Attenuators are classified into two main types: Handheld and Benchtop. Handheld attenuators are portable and suitable for on-the-go applications where mobility is crucial. They are convenient for field technicians and engineers who need to quickly adjust signal strength. On the other hand, Benchtop attenuators are larger and typically used in laboratory settings where precision and stability are key. They are more suitable for controlled and long-term testing environments. Both types cater to different needs and provide flexibility in managing signal attenuation.</p></p>
