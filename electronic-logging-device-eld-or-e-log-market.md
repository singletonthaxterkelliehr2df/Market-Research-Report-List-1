<p><p>The Electronic Logging Device (ELD or E-Log) market consists of three main types: Hours of Service (HOS), Driver Vehicle Inspection Reporting, and Driver Identification. HOS ELDs track and record the hours a driver spends on duty to ensure compliance with regulations. Driver Vehicle Inspection Reporting ELDs allow drivers to electronically report and track vehicle inspections. Driver Identification ELDs help ensure that only authorized drivers are operating a vehicle by requiring driver authentication before the vehicle can be used.</p></p>
