<p><p>OLED lighting driver ICs can be classified into two main types: Mono OLED Driver ICs and Color OLED Driver ICs. Mono OLED Driver ICs are designed for single-color OLED displays, providing the necessary control and power management functions. On the other hand, Color OLED Driver ICs are specifically tailored for color OLED displays, enabling precise control of multiple color channels. Both types of OLED lighting driver ICs play a crucial role in powering and controlling OLED lighting systems for various applications.</p></p>
