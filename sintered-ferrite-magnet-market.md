<p><p>Sintered Ferrite Magnets are divided into two main types: Isotropic and Anisotropic. Isotropic Sintered Ferrite Magnets are uniform in all directions and have equal magnetic properties in every direction. They are commonly used in applications where magnetization direction is not critical. On the other hand, Anisotropic Sintered Ferrite Magnets have a preferred direction of magnetization, making them stronger in one specific direction. They are utilized in applications that require a specific magnetic orientation for optimal performance.</p></p>
