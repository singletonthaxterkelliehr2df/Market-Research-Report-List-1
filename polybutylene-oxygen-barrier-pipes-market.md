<p><strong>The Polybutylene Oxygen Barrier Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H)</li><li>Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polybutylene Oxygen Barrier Pipes are categorized into two main types: Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) and Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R). PB-H pipes are made solely of polybutene-1 polymer, providing high thermal stability and resistance to stress cracking. PB-R pipes, on the other hand, are a blend of polybutene-1 and other copolymers, offering improved flexibility and impact resistance. Both types of Polybutylene Oxygen Barrier Pipes are known for their superior oxygen barrier properties, making them ideal for applications in plumbing and heating systems.</p></p>
