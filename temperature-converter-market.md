<p><strong>The Temperature Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog-to-digital converters (ADCs)</li><li>Digital-to-analog converters (DACs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temperature converter market includes analog-to-digital converters (ADCs) and digital-to-analog converters (DACs). ADCs convert analog temperature measurements into digital signals for processing, while DACs convert digital signals back into analog temperature outputs. These converters are essential components in temperature monitoring and control systems, allowing for accurate measurement and adjustment of temperature levels. The market for ADCs and DACs caters to a wide range of industries, including healthcare, manufacturing, automotive, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
