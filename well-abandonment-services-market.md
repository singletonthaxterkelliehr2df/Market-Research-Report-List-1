<p><ul><li>Temporarily Abandoned</li><li>Shut In</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Well abandonment services encompass various types of activities related to sealing or shutting down oil and gas wells that are no longer in production or use. Temporarily abandoned wells refer to those that are temporarily ceased from operation and can potentially be reactivated in the future. On the other hand, the shut-in market refers to wells that are permanently closed and will not be utilized again due to economic, technical, or environmental reasons. Both types involve well plugging, capping, and sealing to ensure safe containment of fluids and prevent any potential leakage or environmental hazards.</p></p>
