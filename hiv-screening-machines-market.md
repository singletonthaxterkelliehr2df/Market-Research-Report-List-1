<p><p>HIV screening machines come in two main types: Rapid Immunoassay Test and Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Test. Rapid Immunoassay Test machines detect antibodies produced by the immune system in response to HIV infection, providing quick results within minutes. On the other hand, Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Test machines detect viral genetic material, offering faster and more accurate detection of HIV. Both types play a crucial role in HIV screening and early detection efforts, helping to prevent the spread of the virus and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
