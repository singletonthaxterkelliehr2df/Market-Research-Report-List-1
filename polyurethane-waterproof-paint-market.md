<p><ul><li>Crystalline</li><li>Water Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane waterproof paint is available in two main types - Crystalline and Water Emulsion. Crystalline polyurethane waterproof paint forms crystals when it comes in contact with water, providing excellent waterproofing properties. On the other hand, Water Emulsion polyurethane waterproof paint is a water-based solution that forms a protective barrier on the surface, preventing water penetration. Both types of polyurethane waterproof paint are commonly used for waterproofing applications in various industries such as construction, automotive, and marine.</p></p>
