<p><p>High-temperature camera systems can be categorized into two main types: Real Vision Camera System and Thermographic Vision Camera System. The Real Vision Camera System captures visible light images, providing clear and detailed visuals of high-temperature environments. On the other hand, Thermographic Vision Camera System detects infrared radiation emitted by objects to create thermal images. Both systems play vital roles in various industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive by enabling monitoring, measurement, and analysis of high-temperature processes and equipment.</p></p>
