<p><strong>The Steel-string Acoustic Guitar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>O Type</li><li>A Type</li><li>D Type</li><li>G Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel-string acoustic guitars are categorized into different market types based on their body shape and size, which greatly affect the instrument's sound and tone. O Type guitars, also known as Orchestra Type, are versatile and well-suited for various music styles. A Type guitars, or Auditorium Type, offer a balanced tone and comfortable playability, making them popular among fingerstyle guitarists. D Type guitars, or Dreadnought Type, have a louder and more powerful sound projection, ideal for strumming and vocal accompaniment. G Type guitars, or Grand Auditorium Type, combine the qualities of O and D types, providing a balanced sound with enhanced volume and projection.</p></p>
