<p><p>Cross-laminated timber (CLT) and glue-laminated timber (Glulam) are two popular market types within the timber industry. CLT is known for its strength and versatility in construction, with layers of wood stacked perpendicular to each other. Glulam, on the other hand, consists of multiple layers of timber bonded together with strong adhesives. Both products are used in residential and commercial construction for their sustainable and durable properties. The market for CLT and Glulam continues to grow as more architects and builders seek eco-friendly and innovative building materials.</p></p>
