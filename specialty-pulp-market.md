<p><p>Specialty pulp market consists of two main types - softwood pulp and hardwood pulp. Softwood pulp is derived from coniferous trees and is known for its long fibers, making it ideal for products requiring strength and durability, such as packaging materials. On the other hand, hardwood pulp comes from deciduous trees and is valued for its shorter fibers, suitable for producing products like tissues and printing papers. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, meeting specific market demands for high-quality paper and related products.</p></p>
