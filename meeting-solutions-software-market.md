<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>On-Premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Meeting solutions software can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based meeting solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet, allowing for easy scalability, flexibility, and accessibility from anywhere. On-premise meeting solutions are installed and maintained on the organization's own servers, providing complete control over security and customization. Both types have their own advantages and considerations, with cloud-based solutions typically favored for their convenience and cost-effectiveness, while on-premise solutions offer more control and security.</p></p>
