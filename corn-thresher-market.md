<p><p>The corn thresher market consists of two main types: Manual corn threshers and Automatic corn threshers. Manual corn threshers are operated by hand and require physical labor to separate the kernels from the cob. They are usually more cost-effective but slower in processing corn. On the other hand, automatic corn threshers are powered by electricity or fuel and can quickly and efficiently separate the kernels from the cob with minimal manual effort. They are often more expensive but offer higher productivity and convenience.</p></p>
