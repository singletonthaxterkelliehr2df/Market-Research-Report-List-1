<p><p>Condenser lenses are essential components used in optical systems for focusing and directing light. There are two main types of condenser lenses in the market: aspherical type and spherical type. Aspherical lenses offer better image quality by reducing spherical aberrations and distortion, making them ideal for high precision applications. Spherical lenses, on the other hand, are more commonly used due to their lower cost and simpler manufacturing process. Both types have their unique advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the optical system.</p></p>
