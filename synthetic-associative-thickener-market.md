<p><strong>The Synthetic Associative Thickener Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anionic</li><li>Nonionic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic associative thickeners are classified into two types based on their charge - anionic and nonionic. Anionic thickeners have negatively charged particles that help in thickening and stabilizing the formulation. They are commonly used in water-based paints and coatings. Nonionic thickeners, on the other hand, do not have a charge and are versatile in their applications. They are commonly used in personal care products such as lotions and creams. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the rheological properties of various products.</p></p>
