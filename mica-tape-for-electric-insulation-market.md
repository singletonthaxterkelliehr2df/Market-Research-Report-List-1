<p><p>Mica tape for electric insulation is available in different types, with Mica Glass Tape and Mica Polyester Tape being two prominent variations. Mica Glass Tape is made by impregnating glass cloth with mica paper, providing high mechanical strength and good thermal resistance. On the other hand, Mica Polyester Tape combines the flexibility of polyester film with the excellent electrical and thermal properties of mica. Both types of mica tape are widely used in the electrical industry for insulation purposes.</p></p>
