<p><p>Refractory bauxite is categorized into two types based on its alumina content: below 85% and above 85%. Bauxite with alumina content below 85% is typically used in applications where high purity is not a requirement, such as refractory bricks and cement production. On the other hand, bauxite with alumina content above 85% is preferred for applications that demand high purity and quality, such as in the manufacturing of abrasives, ceramics, and specialty refractories.</p></p>
