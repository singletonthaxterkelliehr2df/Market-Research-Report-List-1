<p><strong>The Power Over Etherne Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSE Controllers</li><li>PD Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) controllers are divided into two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) controllers and Powered Device (PD) controllers. PSE controllers are responsible for delivering power to the connected PD devices, while PD controllers manage the power consumption and usage of the devices. PSE controllers are commonly found in Ethernet switches, routers, and midspans, while PD controllers are typically integrated into devices such as IP cameras, wireless access points, and VoIP phones. Both types play a crucial role in enabling the efficient transfer of power and data over Ethernet networks.</p></p>
